Restaurant Description

Eat is a casual dining experience with a fun and energetic staff to make you feel at home. The vibrant decor coupled with the excellent tasty dishes prepared for each guest makes it the perfect spot for lunch or dinner. It is family owned and operated where the owner is on site everyday. Our simple yet delicious menu offers customization for any allergies or preferences. There are daily specials and special additions as this farm to table restaurant has special finds. The ingredients are sourced using local farms and vendors who take pride in their product. Bread is baked here on site, daily. Eat is a local gem visited by neighbors and because of the excellent location gets many visitors who are passing through on their way to upstate New York. Once you Eat at Eat, we can guarantee you will be back to Eat some more
